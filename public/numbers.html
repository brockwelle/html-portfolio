Numeral Prefixes

Some discrepancies between different proposals and standards groups about what comes after Yottabyte

  • 1024 Petabytes = 1 Exabyte
  • 1024 Exabytes = 1 Zettabyte
  • 1024 Zettabytes = 1 Yottabyte [largest approved by Systeme Internationale in 2019
  • 1024 Yottabytes = 1 Xenottabyte [or Ronnabyte]
  • 1024 Xenottabytes = 1 Shilentnobyte [or Quettabyte]
  • 1024 Shilentnobytes = 1 Domegemegrottebyte
  • 1024 Domegemegrottebytes = 1 Icosebyte
  • 1024 Icosebytes = 1 Monoicosebyte
  • 1024 Monoicosebytes = 1 Zenyattamondattabyte
